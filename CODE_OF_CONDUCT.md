# Code of Conduct

This project uses the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct.

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project and its community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- demonstrating empathy and kindness,
- being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences,
- giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback,
- taking responsibility and learning from mistakes,
- focusing on scientific integrity and the health of the project.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- harassment or derogatory comments,
- trolling or intentional disruption,
- publishing private information without explicit permission,
- other conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional research-software community.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior using the private or appropriate reporting paths described in `SECURITY.md` or through the maintainer’s designated contact path.
