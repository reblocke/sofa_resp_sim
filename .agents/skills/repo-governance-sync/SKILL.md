---
name: repo-governance-sync
description: Use when changing contribution docs, issue/PR templates, support/security files, citation metadata, or other repository-governance files. Do not use for numerical or app-layer changes unless they also require governance-file updates.
---

Checklist:

1. Ensure support links point to the correct repository.
2. Ensure security guidance avoids public disclosure for sensitive reports.
3. Ensure no TODO placeholders remain in public-facing governance files.
4. Ensure contribution guidance matches the actual toolchain and commands.
5. Ensure `CITATION.cff` is truthful and minimal.
6. Ensure issue/PR templates collect the information maintainers actually need.
